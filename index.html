We created this website!

posted by Keenan    24th February 2015   

This project to connect BOW students is part of a Products and Marktets class at Olin College. Our team felt that people from the Three College System wanted to interact more, but didn't have a good medium to do so. Students take classes at other schools, and occasionally attend parties, but for the most part remain in their own school's bubble. The current BOW collaboration website isn't user friendly and rarely used. Our team wants to organize fun ways to meet new people, as well as add other features to connect with BOW. Future features might include a craiglist-like service to sell stuff, a business site for networking, a sports organiziation section, and more. We hope you enjoy it!

A Cookie Decoration Extravaganza!

posted by Keenan    5th March 2015  

What a creative and fun event! Cookies of amazing merits came to life on March 5th, and subsequently were devoured by their creators. There is a special beauty about such a short term but delicious piece of artwork. Designs ranged from Starry Night to Yin-Yang symbols to the flag of St. Thomas! By the end of the night, all of the cookies had been decorated and consumed! Trivia questions were also present and tested the brains of those who participated with questions like: 'What was George Washington's first job?', 'Which scientist discovered Uranus?', and 'Which continent touches all lines of longitude?' Thanks to everyone who came and participated! Check out the photos HERE!
